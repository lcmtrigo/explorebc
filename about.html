Wildlife

BC is home to many species of wildlife. Each species varies n size and plays a crucial in role in preserving the beauty of our province. Here are a couple of species that you may encounter during your adventure

  • Great brown bears
  • Wild coyotes
  • Fresh river salmon
  • Stellar Jay
  • Big great moose

The fresh waters

BC is best known for its crystal clear lakes and rivers. 95% of all fresh water in Canada is located in BC. Here a couple of crystal clear lakes and river you should visit during your adventure

  • Cultus Lake
  • North Fraser River
  • Lake Okanagan
  • Harrison Hot Springs
  • Thompson River
