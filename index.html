Go out there and explore

British Columbia is the best province in Canada. It has trees, salmon, bears & lots of nature stuff. Plenty of nature stuff. There is so much out there to explore. You should go outside right now and explore!

Share your experiences exploring this awesome beautiful province on social media by using the hashtag #explorebc

Look up #explorebc on Instagram

Maps

BC has lots of maps because there many roads that lead to special places. Go to your nearest gas station or convenience store to purchase a map of BC.

Roads

There are plenty of roads in BC. Wide and straight, twisty and narrow. Find out which roads you plan to take during your travels.

Camping

There are many places to camp in BC. Visit your nearest outdoor store for all your camping equipment needs.

Take photos

There are many things to see in BC. Make sure you have a camera ready to take photos.
